Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum account balance for a savings account?

The minimum account balance required to open a savings account is £50.

What is the minimum account balance for a current account?

The minimum account balance required to open a current account is £20.

What is the minimum account balance for a fixed deposit account?

The minimum account balance required to open a fixed-deposit account is £200.

How do I apply for a personal loan?

You can apply for a personal loan by visiting any of our banking halls.

Can I open an account online?

No. You can open an account at any of our banking halls.
